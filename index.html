About OAuth Callbacks

This page handles the authentication flow for connecting your social media accounts to Social Aggregator.

Supported Platforms:

  • Spotify: Access your listening history, top tracks, and currently playing music
  • Discord: View your gaming activity, online status, and server presence
  • Strava: Track your workouts, runs, rides, and fitness activities with maps

How it works:

  • Click on a platform to start the OAuth flow
  • Authorize the app to access your data
  • The callback page will process the authorization
  • Your account will be connected to Social Aggregator
